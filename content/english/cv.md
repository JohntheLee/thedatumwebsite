## Research Experience

Undergraduate Thesis in Community Ecology --------- [September 2019 – April 2020]

> Thesis title: “Performances of functional diversity indices to randomized Poisson and binomial data distributions serving as simulations of sampling errors in species abundances”

> Supervisor: Dr. Benjamin Gilbert

> Department of Ecology & Evolutionary Biology, University of Toronto, Toronto, ON

- Conducted experiments on the statistical performances of various functional diversity indices (Community-weighted mean, Functional dispersion, Functional richness, and Functional evenness) when they are confronted with sampling error

- Sampling error was introduced to field-collected datasets using Poisson and binomial distributions

- Programmed the undergraduate thesis project using R (version 3.6.3) and RStudio (version 1.2.1335)

- Developed a specialized R package (Jeonghoon Lee (2020). ErrantAbun: Calculation Of An Abundance Matrix Using A Randomized Distribution. R package version 1.0.1.) to streamline the workflow of the thesis project

- Collaborated with the undergraduate thesis supervisor using Git (version 2.20.1)
